Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Journal: Mood Tracker & Habits respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Journal: Mood Tracker & Habits mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Journal: Mood Tracker & Habits is designed to work primarily with data you create yourself. We may collect the following types of information:

  • Journal entries and notes you create
  • Mood tracking data
  • Habit and task information
  • Calendar events and reminders
  • Albums and media metadata (titles, descriptions)
  • Special dates and life goals

Journal: Mood Tracker & Habits does not collect sensitive personal identifiers such as real names, addresses, or payment information.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide core app functionality, including:

  • Displaying your tasks, habits, and calendar events
  • Tracking moods and journaling history
  • Showing progress and reminders
  • Improving app stability and performance

3. Data Storage

Your data may be stored locally on your device and/or securely in cloud services used by Journal: Mood Tracker & Habits to enable synchronization and backups. Reasonable technical measures are taken to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

Journal: Mood Tracker & Habits does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is never used for advertising purposes.

5. Data Deletion

You may delete your data at any time by removing content inside the app or uninstalling the application.

6. Children's Privacy

Journal: Mood Tracker & Habits is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the app or on this page.
